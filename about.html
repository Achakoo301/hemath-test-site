About HemAth

HemAth is dedicated to empowering displaced Kashmiri women by preserving cultural traditions and providing sustainable income opportunities through artisan craftsmanship.

Through training, mentorship, and community support, HemAth helps women transform traditional skills into meaningful livelihoods while keeping their heritage alive.

Our Vision

To create a global platform where culture, craftsmanship, and community come together to uplift women and preserve heritage for future generations.
